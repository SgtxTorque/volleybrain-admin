Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

Lynx Sports Technology (“Lynx,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the Lynx mobile application and website at thelynxapp.com (“the Service”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is shared, and your rights regarding it.

By using Lynx, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to manage youth sports league participation:

  • Player Information: name, date of birth, school grade, gender, jersey size, jersey number, position, photos, medical conditions, allergies, medications, emergency contacts
  • Parent/Guardian Information: name, email address, phone number, home address, emergency contact details
  • Coach and Staff Information: name, email address, phone number, team assignments, role
  • Financial Information: payment records, registration fee status, installment schedules, transaction history (we do NOT store credit card numbers — see Section 6)
  • Communication Data: chat messages between team members, coaches, parents, and administrators; team announcements; RSVP responses
  • Usage Data: in-app interactions, notification preferences, feature usage, device type, operating system version, crash reports
  • Game and Activity Data: attendance records, game statistics, practice plans, achievement progress, team standings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Manage league registrations, team rosters, and player eligibility
  • Facilitate communication between coaches, parents, players, and administrators
  • Track player attendance, statistics, achievements, and team standings
  • Process registration fees, uniform fees, and other payments
  • Send notifications about schedules, games, payment reminders, and team updates
  • Ensure player safety by giving coaches access to critical medical information
  • Generate reports for league and team administration
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues through crash and error reporting
  • Improve the Service based on aggregated usage patterns

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, and we do not sell personal information to any third party.

3. Device Permissions

Lynx may request the following permissions from your device. You may grant or deny each permission, and you can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings.

  • Camera: To take photos for player profiles, team photos, and event media
  • Photo Library: To upload existing photos to player profiles or team content
  • Microphone: To record voice messages in team chat (optional feature)
  • Calendar: To add team practices, games, and tournaments to your device calendar (optional feature)
  • Push Notifications: To send alerts about schedule changes, messages, RSVP deadlines, and payments
  • Network/Internet: Required for the app to communicate with our servers

Denying optional permissions will not prevent you from using core features of Lynx.

4. Who Can See Your Data

Access to personal information within Lynx is role-based and scoped to the organization (club, league, or program) you belong to:

  • Organization Administrators: May view and manage all data for their organization, including rosters, payments, registrations, and communications
  • Coaches and Team Staff: May view roster details for their assigned teams, including player names, positions, jersey numbers, medical information (for safety), attendance, and statistics
  • Team Managers: May view and manage team-level operations for their assigned teams
  • Parents/Guardians: May view and manage their own children’s profiles, registrations, payments, schedules, and communications
  • Players: May view their own statistics, achievements, team schedule, and team roster

Data is strictly scoped by organization. Users from one organization cannot see data belonging to another organization.

We do NOT share data with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing networks.

5. Third-Party Services We Use

We use the following third-party services to operate Lynx. These services act as data processors on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy practices.

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — Secure cloud database hosting, authentication, and file storage
  • Expo / EAS (expo.dev) — Mobile app build and delivery infrastructure
  • Stripe (stripe.com) — Payment processing. Credit card and bank account details are entered directly on Stripe’s systems; Lynx never sees or stores them
  • Sentry (sentry.io) — Crash and error reporting for diagnosing technical problems
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) and Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — Delivery of push notifications to iOS and Android devices
  • Resend (resend.com) — Transactional email delivery (registration confirmations, password resets, payment receipts)

Each service processes only the minimum data required to perform its function. We do not share data with any third party for their own independent use.

6. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Lynx is designed to support youth sports organizations and necessarily involves information about children under 13. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) through the following practices:

  • Children under 13 are enrolled in Lynx only through a youth sports organization that has an established relationship with the child’s parent or guardian
  • The parent or guardian provides verifiable consent directly to the organization during the registration process, including consent to collect, use, and share information about their child for the purpose of participating in the organization’s activities
  • Lynx does not accept direct self-registration by children. Player accounts are provisioned by a parent through their own Lynx account
  • We collect from children only the information reasonably necessary for participation in the sports program
  • Parents and guardians may review their child’s information, request corrections, request deletion of their child’s data, and revoke consent at any time through the in-app Data Rights section or by contacting us at privacy@thelynxapp.com
  • Revoking consent will remove the child from active rosters and delete their personal information within 30 days, subject to any legal record-keeping requirements

If you believe a child’s information has been collected in Lynx without proper parental consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@thelynxapp.com.

7. Data Storage & Security

  • All personal data is stored in secure, access-controlled cloud databases operated by Supabase
  • All data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access to data is role-based and restricted to authorized users within your organization
  • Authentication uses industry-standard security practices, including password hashing
  • Payment card details are handled entirely by Stripe and are never stored on Lynx servers
  • We regularly review access logs and apply security updates to our infrastructure

No system is perfectly secure. If a data breach occurs that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

8. Data Retention

  • Active player data is retained while the player is enrolled in any current season with an organization
  • Historical game statistics, achievements, and attendance records are retained as part of the player’s career history unless deletion is requested
  • Chat messages are retained for the duration of the season and for a reasonable period afterward to support record-keeping
  • Payment records are retained for at least seven years for tax and audit purposes, as required by law
  • Upon a deletion request, we will delete personal data within 30 days, except where longer retention is required by law (e.g., payment records)

9. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or your child
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information, either through your profile settings or by contacting us
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data collection and processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, use the Data Rights section in your profile, contact your organization administrator, or email privacy@thelynxapp.com. We will respond to all verified requests within 30 days.

10. State-Specific Rights (U.S.)

Residents of certain U.S. states, including California (CCPA/CPRA), Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, and Utah, have additional rights under state privacy laws. These rights may include:

  • The right to know what personal information is collected, used, or disclosed
  • The right to request deletion of personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (Lynx does not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise your state-specific rights, contact privacy@thelynxapp.com. We will respond to verified requests within the timeframes required by your state’s laws.

11. International Users

Lynx is operated from the United States. If you access Lynx from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using Lynx, you consent to this transfer and processing.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through the app or by email. Your continued use of Lynx after a policy update indicates your acceptance of the updated terms.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@thelynxapp.com
  • In the app: Settings → Help & Support
  • Your organization administrator can also assist with data requests on your behalf

We are committed to addressing privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
