07 — Guidelines
Do's & Don'ts
Rules for maintaining visual consistency across the platform.
✓ COLOR
Sky Blue for ALL interactive elements. One accent, used everywhere. Semantic colors only for their meanings.
✕ COLOR
Multiple accent colors on one page. Orange buttons next to purple. Amber as decoration. Semantic colors for non-semantic use.
✓ SURFACES
Cloud → White → Frost. Three layers max. Cards get 1px border + shadow. Inner panels: just background, no border.
✕ SURFACES
Cards on same-color backgrounds. 4+ layers of nesting. Heavy shadows. Glass/blur effects on standard pages.
✓ TYPOGRAPHY
One font family everywhere. Uppercase tracked labels for sections. Navy headings, Slate body. Bold stat numbers in Sky Blue.
✕ TYPOGRAPHY
Google Font imports (Bebas, Rajdhani, DM Sans). Custom @font-face per page. Inline font-family overrides. All-caps headings > 13px.
✓ DARK MODE
Midnight → Charcoal → Graphite. Navy-tinted darks. Same layout, just colors swap. Borders use #2A3545.
✕ DARK MODE
Pure black (#000). Gray-tinted surfaces (bg-slate-800). Different layouts per mode. Bright white on everything.
✓ NAV BAR
Always Navy (#10284C). White logo. Sky Blue/15 active pill. Same in both modes. It's the brand anchor.
✕ NAV BAR
Gradient backgrounds. Colored accent nav bars. Transparent/glass nav. The nav is always solid Navy.
